## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

Design a **modern, clean, editorial-style media kit** for GrowBiz Media LLC, an independent media & content platform based in New York / New Jersey.

The media kit will be used for:
- Brand partnership pitches
- Sponsorship proposals
- Press & PR distribution
- Investor / stakeholder presentations

---

## 2. CREATIVE DIRECTION

### Vibe & References
- **Forbes editorial** — authority, credibility, clean typography
- **Netflix pitch deck** — visual impact, bold layouts, cinematic feel
- **Premium local media brand** — authentic, community-rooted, not corporate

### Core Aesthetic Principles
| Principle | Execution |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Minimal** | Lots of white space, intentional breathing room |
| **Editorial** | Strong typography hierarchy, magazine-quality layout |
| **Human** | Real people, real events, real stories — no stock photos |
| **Impactful** | One main idea per slide/page, visually striking |
| **Professional** | Premium feel without "marketing agency" aesthetics |

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep Blue (`#1A2B4A` or similar)
- **Secondary:** White / Off-white (`#FFFFFF` / `#F8F9FA`)
- **Accent:** Mustard / Gold (`#D4A017` or similar)
- **Text:** Dark charcoal for body copy

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Helvetica Neue, or custom)
- **Body:** Clean, highly readable sans-serif
- **Accent/Quotes:** Elegant serif for pull quotes (optional editorial touch)

## 4. DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

- **Primary:** Digital PDF (landscape, 16:9 ratio — presentation-friendly)
- **Secondary:** Portrait A4 version for print (if feasible)

### Layout Rules
- ✅ One main idea per slide

- ✅ White space is your friend
- ✅ Real photography ONLY (events, interviews, BTS)
- ❌ No stock photos
- ❌ No cluttered layouts
- ❌ No long text blocks
- ❌ No generic marketing agency templates
