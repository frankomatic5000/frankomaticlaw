## 2. WHO WE ARE

GrowBiz Media is an independent media platform dedicated to documenting, elevating, and distributing stories that drive local economies and strengthen communities.

We focus on small businesses, entrepreneurs, and community-driven initiatives, giving visibility to the people who are actively building and moving their environments forward.

Through our bilingual approach, we connect cultures, audiences, and opportunities across New York and New Jersey.

---

## 3. OUR MISSION

To give visibility to small businesses, entrepreneurs, and community leaders — transforming real stories into meaningful content that generates connection, recognition, and growth.

### OUR FOCUS

We believe that strong communities are built by local businesses and the people behind them.

That's why our work is centered on:
- Small business visibility
- Entrepreneurial journeys
- Community-driven events
- Local economic impact
- Cultural connection and representation

### WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

While traditional media focuses on large-scale narratives, GrowBiz is committed to local impact.

- We document real people — not just events.
- We highlight stories with purpose — not just content.
- We create visibility where it matters most: at the community level.

---

## 4. ABOUT IMIGROU

**IMIGROU** is our Portuguese-language media channel, created to amplify the voices and experiences of the Brazilian immigrant community in the United States.

Through interviews, storytelling, and community coverage, IMIGROU highlights real journeys, challenges, and achievements, building connection, identity, and representation.

Together, GrowBiz and IMIGROU form a media ecosystem that informs, connects, and elevates.
