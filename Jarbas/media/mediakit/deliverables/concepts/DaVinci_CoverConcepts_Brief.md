# GROWBIZ MEDIA KIT — DaVinci Cover Concepts Brief

## Project Context
Design a modern, clean, editorial-style media kit for GrowBiz Media LLC (Karine's media brand).

## Creative Direction
- **Inspiration:** Forbes editorial + Netflix pitch deck + premium local media aesthetic
- **Color Palette:** Deep blue (#1A2B4A), white, mustard/gold (#D4A017) accents
- **Philosophy:** Minimal, clean, lots of white space
- **Photography:** Real photos ONLY (events, interviews, behind-the-scenes)
- **Format:** PDF landscape 16:9, 12 slides total

## Design Rules
- One main idea per slide
- Max 30% text coverage per slide
- Large, bold numbers for stats
- White space is your friend
- Real photography ONLY
- No stock photos, no cluttered layouts, no generic templates

---

## COVER CONCEPTS (3 Options)

### CONCEPT A: "The Editorial Cover"
**Vibe:** High-end magazine front page (Forbes meets Vanity Fair)
**Layout:**
- Full-bleed hero photograph of Karine at an event or interview (real photo, candid moment)
- "GROWBIZ MEDIA LLC" in large, confident serif typeface (Playfair Display or similar) anchored at top-left
- Thin gold rule line separating brand name from tagline
- Tagline: "Your voice. Your stage. Your most professional version." — small, elegant, bottom-left
- "New York • New Jersey" — micro-text, bottom-right in gold
- Deep blue gradient overlay at top (20% opacity) for text legibility
- Mustard/gold accent on a single letter or graphic element

**Why this works:** Establishes authority immediately. Feels like you've arrived.

---

### CONCEPT B: "The Minimalist Statement"
**Vibe:** Apple Keynote meets boutique agency deck
**Layout:**
- 80% white space
- "GROWBIZ" in massive, bold sans-serif (Montserrat or similar) — centered, occupying top 40% of slide
- "MEDIA LLC" directly beneath, smaller, lighter weight, same family
- Single thin gold horizontal rule centered between brand name and tagline
- Tagline centered below in small, refined italics
- Deep blue footer bar (5% height) with "New York • New Jersey" in white
- No imagery on cover — save the photography for interior spreads
- One subtle gold dot or geometric accent

**Why this works:** Confidence through restraint. The absence of visual noise screams premium.

---

### CONCEPT C: "The Human Connection"
**Vibe:** Netflix documentary title card + community-focused warmth
**Layout:**
- Split composition: left 40% deep blue solid panel, right 60% full-bleed photo collage (3 overlapping images of events, interviews, behind-the-scenes — real photos only)
- On the blue panel (left): "GROWBIZ MEDIA" in white, stacked vertically, large scale, sans-serif
- "LLC" in mustard/gold, smaller, aligned right of "MEDIA"
- Tagline in white, small, bottom of blue panel
- Right side: Photo collage with soft feathered edges, no hard borders
- "New York • New Jersey" — small, gold, overlaid on photo area at bottom
- Optional: single gold accent line connecting blue panel to photo area

**Why this works:** Leads with the human story. Perfect for Karine's mission of giving visibility to real people.

## Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1920×1080px (16:9 landscape)
- **Color Space:** RGB (web/digital) + CMYK conversion note for print
- **Safe Margin:** 60px from all edges
- **Typography:**
  - Primary: Playfair Display (editorial serif)
  - Secondary: Montserrat (clean sans-serif)
  - Accent: Cormorant Garamond (elegant italic for quotes/taglines)
- **Export:** High-res PNG (300dpi), PDF-compatible vector paths where possible
