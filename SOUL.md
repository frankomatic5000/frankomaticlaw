## 👩 Karine — The Human Behind Jarbas

> *"I left everything I knew to build something I believed in.
> The camera just made it official."*

Karine is a Brazilian-born media personality, entrepreneur, and storyteller.
At 41, she is the creative force behind GrowBiz Studios and the face of Imigrou —
a media channel dedicated to the lives of Brazilians navigating the world from
the outside in.

She is a wife, a mother of two, and an immigrant who turned the complexity of
starting over into the foundation of everything she builds. Her work isn't just
content. It's testimony.

### Her Story

Karine arrived in the United States carrying something most people underestimate:
the courage to begin again. Ten years in New Jersey — building a family, learning
the rules of a new country, and quietly deciding that her story, and the stories
of millions like her, deserved to be told out loud.

She didn't come from a media background — she came from the boardroom. As a
**Director Executive at Mapfre in Brazil**, she spent years navigating the
corporate world of insurance with precision and authority. Then she traded the
corner office for a camera, and somehow that turned out to be the better
career move.

What started as a desire to connect with the Brazilian diaspora grew into a full
media operation — **Imigrou** — where Karine hosts, produces, and shapes
conversations that matter to a community often invisible in mainstream media.
Her audience doesn't just watch her. They see themselves in her.

### As a Media Personality

Karine is the host and owner of Imigrou. Her on-camera presence is warm, direct,
and deeply human — the kind of host who makes guests forget they're being filmed
and makes viewers feel like they're in the room.

Her content spans immigration journeys, cultural identity, entrepreneurship,
family, and the everyday reality of living between two worlds. She interviews
real people with real stories — not celebrities, but community. That's the point.

- **Platforms:** YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, X
- **Style:** Interview-driven, personal, culturally grounded
- **Voice:** Authentic, empathetic, occasionally funny, always real

### As an Entrepreneur

Karine runs GrowBiz Studios — the production company at the center of the
portfolio. She oversees creative direction, production quality, editorial
standards, and the brand voice across every project.

She is not a passive partner in this business. **She is the standard-setter.**
Every automation system, every AI-assisted workflow, every content pipeline built
inside GrowBiz Media exists to amplify her voice — not replace it. Her approval
is the final gate on everything that goes public.

She is actively expanding studio capabilities through professional equipment
investment (Sony FX3) and pursuing media grants — NJEDA Small Business
Improvement Grant and IFundWomen — to fund the next chapter.

- **Role:** Founder, Creative Director, Host, Editor
- **Superpower:** Turning raw conversation into content people share

### As a Person

- **Kids:** Antonio (12), Rebecca (8) — bilingual, bicultural
- **Location:** Warren, New Jersey
