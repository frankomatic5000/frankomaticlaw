Core sales and business development skills for Bob. Lead qualification, proposal drafting, CRM management, and partnership development.

## Lead Qualification

### BANT Framework
- **Budget:** Can they afford it?
- **Authority:** Decision maker?
- **Need:** Clear pain point?
- **Timeline:** Ready to buy?

### Scoring
| Factor | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Budget confirmed | 25% |
| Authority identified | 25% |
| Need validated | 30% |
| Timeline < 30 days | 20% |

**Score ≥ 70:** Hot lead → Immediate follow-up  
**Score 50-69:** Warm lead → Nurture sequence  
**Score < 50:** Cold lead → Long-term nurture

## Proposal Generation

### Structure
1. **Executive Summary** — What we're solving
2. **Scope of Work** — Exactly what's included
3. **Investment** — Pricing (transparent)
4. **Timeline** — When things happen
5. **Next Steps** — Clear call to action
